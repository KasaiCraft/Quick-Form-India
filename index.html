About QuickForm India

Our Mission

QuickForm India is designed to simplify the process of filling government forms for Indian citizens. We understand how time-consuming and repetitive it can be to fill multiple government forms with the same information.

How It Works

Our platform allows you to enter your personal details once and automatically generate properly formatted PDFs for various government applications including PAN card, Aadhaar updates, passport applications, and more.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our top priority. All data processing happens locally in your browser. We do not store any personal information on our servers. Your sensitive data never leaves your device.

Disclaimer

QuickForm India is not affiliated with any government agency. This is an independent service designed to help citizens prepare their forms more efficiently. Always verify the requirements with official government websites before submission.

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

QuickForm India operates on a privacy-first principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information you enter into our forms. All data processing happens locally in your browser.

How We Use Information

  • Form data is processed locally to generate PDFs
  • No personal data is sent to our servers
  • No user accounts or profiles are created
  • No tracking of individual users

Data Storage

Your personal information is temporarily stored in your browser's memory only during the form filling process. Once you close the browser or navigate away, all data is automatically cleared.

Third-Party Services

We may use anonymous analytics to understand website usage patterns. No personal information is shared with third parties.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies only for essential website functionality. No personal data is stored in cookies.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page.
